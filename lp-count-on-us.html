Dental Insurance Welcome

We realize the importance of taking care of your teeth, and we welcome dental insurance as a partner in making exceptional dentistry affordable. If you are fortunate enough to have PPO dental insurance, call us. We work closely with all the leading plans, and our team will help you understand and maximize your benefits.

Note: If you do not see your plan, do not worry. Call. We welcome other plans.

No Insurance? No Worries!

We also work with third-party lenders, such as CareCredit®, and offer in-office payment plans for patients without dental insurance to help provide you with payment options that meet your budget. For more information about our financial options for dental care, call our office and schedule a visit with our dentists, Dr. Michel Damerji and Dr. Monaf Alyassi.
